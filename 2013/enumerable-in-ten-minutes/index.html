Enumerable in 10 Minutes

by Pete Nicholls on 17 Oct 2013 at Christchurch Ruby

This upcoming talk will be given on October 17th at Newbie Night.

Enumerable is a fascinating mixin. Its use makes for unusual, characteristic, and expressive Ruby code.

You'll learn:

  • Why Rubyists never use for
  • Why spaceships are awesome
  • Stupid tricks with lists
  • How to cook teppanyaki
  • How to count the uncountable
