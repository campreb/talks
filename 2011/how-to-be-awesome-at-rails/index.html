How to be awesome at Rails

by Ryan Bigg on 15 Nov 2011 at Christchurch Ruby

Original announcement:

We'll be welcoming Ryan Bigg from Sydney, who has agreed to give a short talk to the group during his visit to Christchurch. Ryan is something of a superstar in the Rails community. He is the co-author, along with Yehuda Katz, of the book Rails 3 in Action, and was the recipient of a Ruby Hero award in 2011 for his work on documenting Rails.
